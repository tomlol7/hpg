phenotypes

Find your phenotype from a picture!

[GitHub] – by @sidstuff

Are you a user of r/phenotypes? Been to humanphenotypes.net and curious about your own ethnic background? This website takes a user-submitted photo and uses facial recognition to compare its similarity to the averaged samples given on Human Phenotypes. All processing is done within the browser and your data never goes to a server, so your privacy is guaranteed.
